My essay

The Lantern Festival fell into the wine glass of the full moon, and I was also favored by unexpected beauty. Picking up the wine glass, I found a ray of brilliant light shining in the lanterns unique to the Lantern Festival, and suddenly my heart precipitated the world's finest nectar.

The streets are full of festive celebrations, and the red lanterns swaying in the wind flamboyantly bask in the happiness of the owners. The square is full of couples who are beating and clamoring, dancing and having fun. Their affectionate eyes and restless hands and feet are as hot as Valentine's Day. I want to pick up the Yuye wine glass filled with Lantern Festival, and drink it up in one gulp.

The lively Lantern Festival cannot tolerate a little bit of sadness and loneliness. I didn't make up my mind to have a carnival until I heard the distant singing from the KTV.
